# OrganizerHQ Tutorial

OrganizerHQ is Major League Hacking's event hosting platform. You will use OHQ to collect registrations and check-ins for your Hack Day.&#x20;

Completing your Hack Day application should result in a fully ready event page. No extra work necessary!

Have any questions or run into any problems while setting up your event? Email us at hackdays@mlh.io and we will help you out.
