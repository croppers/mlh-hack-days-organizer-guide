# Hack Day Planning Timeline

#### 1-2 Months Before

* [Apply to host](https://organize.mlh.io/host/google-gemini-hack-days)
* [Identify date & time](https://guide.mlh.io/general-information/finding-the-date-and-purpose) - remember, Hack Day events should be 12 hours or less (weâ€™ve approved events as short as four hours). Remember to target at least one month out from your application date.
* [Secure a venue](https://guide.mlh.io/general-information/locking-down-a-venue)
* [Start outreach](https://guide.mlh.io/general-information/marketing-your-event/promoting-your-event)

#### 2-3 Weeks Before

* [Finalize and order from your food vendors](https://guide.mlh.io/general-information/event-logistics/working-with-food-vendors)
* [Plan event logistics](https://guide.mlh.io/general-information/event-logistics)

* [Draft schedule](https://guide.mlh.io/general-information/hackathon-scheduling/detailed-run-of-show)

#### 1 Week Before

* [Send reminders](https://guide.mlh.io/general-information/managing-registrations/sending-reminders)
* Finalize headcount
* Receive MLH swag
* [Prepare emergency plan](https://guide.mlh.io/general-information/event-logistics/prepare-your-emergency-plan)

#### Day of the Event

* [Set up your event](https://guide.mlh.io/general-information/event-logistics/set-up-your-event)
* [Check-in process](https://guide.mlh.io/general-information/managing-registrations/check-in-process)
* Welcome attendees
* [Distribute meals](https://guide.mlh.io/general-information/event-logistics/distributing-meals)
* Facilitate projects
* [Run workshops/mini-events](https://guide.mlh.io/general-information/day-of-logistics-and-hacker-experience/workshops)
* [Judging and submissions](https://guide.mlh.io/general-information/judging-and-submissions)
* Announce winners

#### After the Event

* Submit receipts
* Provide feedback
* Encourage reflection
